Sorcery Fight is heavily inspired by anime/manga Jujutsu Kaisen.

The mod adds:
Four innate techniques, Limitless, Shrine, Private Pure Love Train, and Vessel,
each with a total of 15 cursed techniques, 5 passive techniques, and 4 domain expansions.
as well as 2 custom boss domains!

NOTE: This mod requires Calamity Mod and its dependencies in order to install.
